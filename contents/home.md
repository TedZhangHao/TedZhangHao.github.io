### About me 🙌
Hi there 👋! My name is **Hao Zhang (张昊)**. I am currently pursuing my Master's Degree in Computer Science at **Johns Hopkins University**, where I also work at Center for Language and Speech Processing ([CLSP](https://www.clsp.jhu.edu/)) under the supervision by [Prof. Laureano Moro-Velazquez](https://pages.jh.edu/lmorove1/index.html).
